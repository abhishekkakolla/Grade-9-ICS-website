TLDR

  1. CPU Overview

    • The CPU facilitates all tasks of computer; is solely responsible for executing commands

    • Early CPUs only have one processor; todays CPUs are actually a collection of individual processors working together

  2. CPU History

    • Nicola Tesla patented a logic circuit in 1903; these logic "gates" were the basis of the CPU

    • The first transistor was invented in 1947; operates similarly to Tesla's logic gates

    • Transistors are the building blocks of the CPU; enables it to perform logical operations

    • Intel built the first ever microprocessor (CPU) in 1970

  3. Today's CPU

    • AMD and Intel are two major CPU producers

    • They produce some of the most popular models today

  4. The CPU's Future

    • CPU processing speed and capability has grown exponentially in past decades

    • But they will eventually reach their physical limits; experts predict this will happen in the 2020s

    • Improvements in software, such as the cloud and wireless communication may be the necessary to improve efficiency of computers

    • Recently, quantum computing has become a hot topic; quantum computers exploit quantum mechanics to operate much faster than classical computers

    • Quantum computers may be the future of computing
